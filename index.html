Instructions

There is a raid of Orcs heading
into the Castle and the King
requested you to save
the citizens!

Don't let the Orcs hit you,
they hurt! Shoot them down
before they get past you and into
the castle.

Press the space bar to shoot
your arrows and navigate with
the arrow keys.

Collect falling arrows and
potions if you're running low!
